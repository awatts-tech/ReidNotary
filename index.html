About Us

Reid Mobile Notary LLC provides notary, signing agent and other professional services. We service Detroit, Michigan and surrounding areas. We aim to provide reliable, professional and efficient service to meet our client's needs.

Services.

Why consider a Traveling/Mobile Notary?

"We are available when you need us". We will travel to your home, business, hospitals, nursing homes or anywhere you would like to meet at your convenience to notarize your documents. Reid Mobile Notary Services, LLC is certified, background checked, bonded and insured. We carry bond and Errors & Omissions insurance. We are members of the National Notary Association and offer mobile notary services. We accept email, fax and overnight documents. All documents are printed on a laser printer.
